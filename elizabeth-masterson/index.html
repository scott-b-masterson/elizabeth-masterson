How We Work

The Design Process

A calm, considered path from first conversation to final reveal.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We talk through how you live, your style, your timeline, and your budget. Every project begins by listening.

  2. 02

    Concept & Mood

    Mood boards, palettes, and design direction — so we agree on the feeling of the space before we build it.

  3. 03

    Design Development

    Space planning, floor plans, and full FF&E specifications in SketchUp, AutoCAD, and Revit.

  4. 04

    Sourcing & Procurement

    Furnishings, lighting, and materials sourced from trusted brands and ordered, tracked, and managed for you.

  5. 05

    Install & Styling

    Delivery, placement, and the finishing layers — art, accessories, and the details that make it feel like home.

  6. 06

    The Reveal

    You walk into a finished space that's beautiful, functional, and unmistakably yours.

About

Meet Elizabeth

Elizabeth Masterson is an interior designer based in metro Detroit, creating beautiful, livable homes for clients across the country. She founded Masterson Design Style in 2017, specializing in space planning, material selection, and full-home renovations.

Today she designs with the Wayfair Design Services team — creating rooms for AllModern, Joss & Main, Birch Lane, and Perigold — and previously designed with Modsy and Ethan Allen. Her work blends quiet luxury, organic modern texture, soft glamour, and timeless traditional details into spaces that are aspirational yet completely at home.
